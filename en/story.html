What guides us

Principles that are
non-negotiable.

01

Rigor before speed

Our priority was never to be the fastest. It was to be the most reliable for those who need to publish with evidence.

02

Full traceability

Every analysis must be auditable. Model, version, prompt, temperature — everything documented for reproducibility.

03

Built by those who use it

No feature was added without being needed in a real research project. This ensures every detail matters.

04

Model neutrality

We do not advocate for any LLM. We provide the layer that allows choosing the best model for each task.
