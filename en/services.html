What we offer

Document analysis
that scales with you.

Large-scale analysis

🔬

Systematic Reviews

Pre-analysis, extraction, and synthesis of evidence from corpora of hundreds of articles, with full traceability.

🎙️

Interview Analysis

Categorization, coding, and synthesis of qualitative transcripts at scale, with researcher-defined criteria.

🗂️

Diverse Document Analysis

Extraction and organization of data from institutional documents, reports, forms, and archives of any nature.

📚

Large-Scale Corpus Analysis

Processing of dozens to hundreds of documents with the same criteria, model, and traceability.

🔁

Multi-Model Analysis

Running the same analysis with GPT, Gemini, and Claude in parallel for validation and result comparison.

Team services

The team directly conducts verification analyses and provides advisory for research design, execution, and dissemination.

🗂️

Title and Abstract Screening

Selection and filtering of articles by titles, abstracts, and keywords — essential for systematic reviews with large reference volumes.

🔍

Adequacy Crosscheck

Verification of correspondence between what was produced by the analysis and the original corpus documents — ensuring fidelity and coverage.

📄

Plagiarism Crosscheck

Identification of textual similarity risks, paraphrase adequacy, and citation accuracy in relation to original sources.

🧭

Research Design and Conduct

Advisory for methodological structuring of the analysis — defining criteria, categories, variables, and analytical workflow.

⚙️

Prompts, Data, and Dissemination

Prompt development, data organization and categorization, and support for preparing results for dissemination.
