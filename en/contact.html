Frequently asked questions

Before reaching
out.

Is the software already available?

It depends on availability. Access is granted by request. Get in touch to check current availability and discuss your case.

Can I use it for my dissertation or thesis?

Yes, but by request. Get in touch describing your project — we will jointly evaluate the best way to make it work.

Do I need to know how to code to use it?

No. FullCorpus AI was built for researchers, not developers. No technical knowledge is required.

What is the usage model?

Conditions are defined case by case, in conversation with the team. Get in touch to understand how FullCorpus AI can fit your project.

Can I use it with my own data?

Yes. The tool processes data in general — documents, interviews, reports, articles. For sensitive data, we jointly assess appropriate precautions.

What are the cross-checking services?

We offer two types of verification: adequacy crosscheck, which verifies whether the produced content faithfully corresponds to the original corpus documents; and plagiarism crosscheck, which identifies textual similarity risks and citation adequacy in relation to sources.
